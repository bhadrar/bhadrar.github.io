-~-_-~-Extracurriculars-~-_-~-

My extracurricular subjects are mainly focused on music. I participate in three kinds of voice, and play the piano and violin.

  1. Voice - 9 years

    • Choir
    • Carnatic (An Indian style of singing)
    • Hindustani (Another Indian style of singing)

  2. Piano - 9 years

    • I have participated in the Certificate of Merit (a musical evaluation test that includes technique, theory, and the performance of musical pieces from different eras) for piano since 3rd grade and I am now in level 6. Last year, I received Branch and State Honors for excellence in performance and evaluation.

  3. Violin - 7 years

    • I have participated in the Certificate of Merit for violin since 7th grade and I am now in level 6. Last year, I received Branch and State Honors for excellence in performance and evaluation.

Overall, these extracurriculars have helped me in many ways to grow as a person. Because I have to regularly practice and take evaluations, playing these instruments and singing have really helped teach me the importance of time management. My extracurriculars have also taught me that if I work hard, I can reach my goals. In addition, they have taught me to never give up if I am struggling with learning a piece, and this applies to my everyday life too. In these aspects, having these extracurricular activites has helped build my character as a student and as a person.
